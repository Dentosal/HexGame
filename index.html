HexGame 0.2.2

What?

HexGame is a set of simple conversion games, where you convert different formats to hexadecimal and back.

How?

Select the game and difficulty from the top bar. Then press enter to start a game. Then type in your answer and press enter. If your answer was incorrect, the game tells you the correct answer.

Why?

It's fun, and somewhat useful.
